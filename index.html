About Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate is a fast-paced, non-contact team sport played with a flying disc. Combining elements of soccer, basketball, and football, ultimate is played on a field where two teams try to score by catching the disc in the opposing end zone.

What makes Ultimate special:

  • Spirit of the Game: Self-officiated with a focus on sportsmanship and fair play
  • Inclusive: Co-ed teams welcome players of all genders and skill levels
  • Athletic & Strategic: Combines speed, endurance, and tactical thinking
  • Community: Build lasting friendships through shared passion for the sport
