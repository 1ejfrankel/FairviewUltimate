Registration Requirements

Before You Register:

  • USA Ultimate Membership: All players must have an active USA Ultimate Youth or Affiliate membership and liability waiver.
  • Medical Form: Complete the USAUltimate Medical Release form.
  • Join the Mailing List: https://groups.google.com/g/fairview-hs-ultimate-frisbee (put player name as reason for joining).

Please complete all fields in the registration form below. Both the medical form and an active USA Ultimate membership are required to participate in practices and games.
