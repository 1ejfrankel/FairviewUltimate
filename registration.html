Registration Requirements

Before You Register:

  • USA Ultimate Membership: All players must have an active USA Ultimate Youth or Affiliate membership
  • Medical Form: Complete the USAUltimate Medical Release form, found here (https://usaultimate.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Medical-Authorization-V4.1.form_.pdf)

Please complete all fields in the registration form below. Both the medical form and USA Ultimate membership number are required to participate in practices and games.
